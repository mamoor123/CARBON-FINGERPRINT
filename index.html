📊 How we calculated your footprint

Emission factors are based on IPCC AR6 Working Group III, supplemented by peer-reviewed sources including Poore & Nemecek (2018, Science), the IEA World Energy Outlook 2023, and the FAO.

  • Transport: EPA average (4.6t/car/yr), ICCT transit factors
  • Diet: Poore & Nemecek 2018 lifecycle analysis (plant-based: 1.5t, high-meat: 3.3t)
  • Energy: IEA electricity emission factors by grid type
  • Flights: ICAO carbon calculator (transatlantic ≈ 1.6t CO₂)
  • Shared (1.5t): Government services, infrastructure, military — from Our World in Data

Global average (4.7t): World Bank 2023. 1.5°C target (2.3t): IPCC AR6 WG3 carbon budget pathway. US average (14.5t): Global Carbon Project 2023.

Climate data: NASA GISS Surface Temperature Analysis (GISTEMP v4), Mauna Loa CO₂ records, NOAA sea level data.
